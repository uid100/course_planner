Plan for success

Planning is an important part of any project execution. You may be have heard Plan your work, work your plan. We can probably all recall tasks that have started with no planning or preparation and the predictable results, or lack of results.

   "Planning is an unnatural process; it is much more fun to do something.

   The nicest thing about not planning is that failure comes as a complete surprise, rather than being preceded by a period of worry and depression."

- Sir John Harvey Jones

Starting without a plan is often equivalent to not starting at all.

If you don't know where you're going, any road will get you there.
- Lewis Carroll

The problem is that planning done badly or not tracked and used can be worse than not planning in the first place.
