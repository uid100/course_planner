Data-centric design

A data-centric approach to solution programming is an approach that places a strong emphasis on the importance of data in designing, developing, and implementing software solutions. This approach focuses on the organization, management, and utilization of data to meet specific business needs or solve particular problems.

In a data-centric approach, the design of the solution is based on a thorough understanding of the data and the relationships between different data elements. The approach also involves identifying the various types of data required for the solution, defining data models and schemas, and selecting the appropriate data storage and retrieval mechanisms.

The data-centric approach recognizes that data is the most valuable asset in most organizations and applications. As such, it should be treated as a primary consideration in the development of software solutions. By prioritizing data in this way, the approach helps to ensure that solutions are optimized for data storage, management, and analysis, ultimately leading to better decision-making and improved business outcomes.
