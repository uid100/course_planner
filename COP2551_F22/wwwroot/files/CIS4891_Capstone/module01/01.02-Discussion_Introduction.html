Purpose:

  • Since we don't have the opportunity to go around the classroom and have people introduce themselves, we're going to do an online introduction and share a little bit about ourselves.

 

Directions: (50 pts)

  • Add your picture to your Canvas profile. From the Canvas main menu (typically on the upper left side of your screen), select Account and go to Settings and upload a picture. Canvas Student Guide
  • Click reply below, and add a post introducing yourself to the class. Something about yourself, your academic and leisure interests.
  • This advanced level course, occurs late in your undergraduate path. At this stage, most students will be working on closing this current chapter and likely starting others. The paths leading to the prerequisite courses for this one has some variability built into it. Everyone's journey has been unique and I am interested in yours.
  • As part of your introduction, tell us about the courses you have studied, at this school, at others, or in any other professional or other area. How would you summarize and describe what you are learning or have learned? What did you find interesting? What do you wish you had spent more time learning?
  • If you could advise other students starting down the same path, what would you recommend, and what do you wish you had done differently?
  • This is a small class, at the end of a long journey. Feel free to respecfully interact with others in the discussion. It is not a requirement. Remember to join our Discord channel to say hello.
  • Organize your thoughts. Spelling, grammar and sentence structure matter.

As always, posts and replies should be thoughtful and respectful.
