Solution Design

Simplifying Complex Ideas

Once the need has been identified and the concept is well-defined, we transition to the Design phase. Sometimes, on large projects, it is recommended to add Subject Matter Expert (SME) consultants to the project team, or even to temporarily reassign a small group of engineers to immerse themselves in the operational environment in order to have a clear understanding of how the solution will be used.

Once the problem is clearly understood, the next objective is to try and deconstruct complex systems into smaller, simpler, more manageable components.

In this module, you will create design artifacts intended to clarify and communicate your design and deconstruct the solution into manageable components.
