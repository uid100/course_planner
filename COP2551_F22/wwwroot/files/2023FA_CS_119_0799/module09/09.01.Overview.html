with Graphical User Interfaces

    Content:

  • The actions that GUI components can initiate
  • Designing graphical user interfaces
  • The steps to developing an event-driven application
  • Threads and multithreading
  • Creating animation

 

 

A bit of history: The GUI approach was originally developed at Xerox’s Palo Alto Research Center (PARC). However, Xerox’s top management failed to see the importance of these ideas, and the concepts were shown to several people, including Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak (Apple Computer), who promptly used these techniques in the Macintosh. Bill Gates of Microsoft also implemented similar ideas into Windows, and the rest is history!
