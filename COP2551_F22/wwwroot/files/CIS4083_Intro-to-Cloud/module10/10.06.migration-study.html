Migrating to the Cloud

Search the web for at least three case studies of companies that migrated traditional business operations to cloud services.

List the companies you found with resource links. Include the date when the information was published if you can find it.

Respond to the following with 1 to 3 paragraphs each. Your answer to each may be specific to one, or more than one of the case studies that you found. Indicate which in your answers.

  1. Why did the organization decide to migrate to the cloud?
  2. What were the primary business drivers for the migration?
  3. What cloud platform or service did they choose?
  4. What were the biggest challenges during the migration process?
  5. How did the organization approach the migration? Did they use a phased or big-bang approach?
  6. What were the benefits realized after the migration was complete?
  7. Were there any unexpected outcomes or issues during or after the migration?
  8. How did the organization manage security and compliance in the cloud environment?
  9. What was the impact of the cloud migration on the organization's IT staff?
  10. What advice would the organization give to others considering a cloud migration?

Submit your work as a Word document (.docx).

Be sure to review your work for spelling, grammar, and punctuation, or have someone review it for you before submitting.
