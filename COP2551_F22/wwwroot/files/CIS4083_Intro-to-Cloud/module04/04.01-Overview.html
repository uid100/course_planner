Flexible Architecture

Cloud services are available to provide a variety of services and organizational needs. The Cloud environment is flexible and scalable. Cloud architecture represents a complex suite of capabilities supported by a complex array of hardware.

A cloud deployement model is defined according to where the infrastructure for the deployment resides and who has control over that infrastructure.
